1.2.17. patsy.Term¶

class patsy.Term(factors)[source]¶

The interaction between a collection of factor objects.

This is one of the basic types used in representing formulas, and corresponds to an expression like "a:b:c" in a formula string. For details, see formulas and expert-model-specification.

Terms are hashable and compare by value.

Attributes:

factors¶

A tuple of factor objects.
